Who We Are

Built on craft,

Frame Studio is a creative design agency based in Mozambique. We believe every brand deserves a visual identity that tells its story with clarity and impact.

From startups to established businesses, we bring a sharp, detail-oriented approach to every project — delivering work that is both aesthetically bold and strategically intentional.

Our international mindset paired with a deep local understanding allows us to create work that connects across cultures and markets.
